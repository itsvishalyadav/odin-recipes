Recipe Name: Kaju Paneer

Description

Kaju Paneer is what happens when royalty meets indulgence. Paneer cubes chilling in a creamy, nutty, rich gravy made from cashews, butter, and bold spices. It's the kind of dish that makes you question why you ever ate anything else.

This isn’t your regular “dal-chawal” night. This is wedding buffet-level, festival-approved, creamy seduction on a plate. It doesn’t care about your calorie count. It came to slay, not to save.

Ingredients

  • Paneer cubes – The MVP of North Indian cuisine.
  • Cashews – Soaked and blended into creamy glory.
  • Onion & tomato puree – The saucy soul.
  • Fresh cream – Because moderation is a myth.
  • Butter – The golden life force of Indian food.
  • Garam masala, turmeric, chili powder – For flavor bombs.

Steps:

  1. Sauté onions and tomatoes, blend with soaked cashews into a smooth gravy.
  2. Cook the gravy in butter, add spices, and stir like a fancy potion.
  3. Drop in paneer cubes like royalty entering a palace.
  4. Pour cream and let the whole thing simmer into luxury.
  5. Garnish with chopped coriander and a few extra kajus for flex.
  6. Serve hot with naan, rice, or just a spoon straight into your mouth.
